Research

I'm interested in Artificial Intelligence, and topics at the intersection of Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning. Previously, I have worked under Dr. Dipankar Das on Hate Speech Classification and Question Generation.

Hate speech detection and classification from tweets
Dr. Dipankar Das,  Souvik Mondal,  Antariksha Ray, 

We extracted different features like n-gram, sentiment, word and sentence vectors from tweets. We then performed a comparative study on the features and their combinations to detect and classify tweets into three classes - neutral, offensive and hate speech. After fine-tuning the best-performing model, we developed a graphical user interface for the system to take a tweet as input and display the scores and the output class.
Question Generation framework for simple and complex sentences
Rubel Das,  Antariksha Ray,  Souvik Mondal,  Dr. Dipankar Das

We employed various generation and disambiguation rules for formulating questions from simple and complex sentences. In addition to building a question generation system along with pre and post processing modules, we also developed and integrated a clause identification module to deal with complex sentences. The proposed three-graded rating scheme along with error analysis showed that the system achieves ~80% accuracy for various wh-questions (who, what, where etc.) along with quantitative types (how much and how many).
